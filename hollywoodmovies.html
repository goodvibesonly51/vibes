WHAT ARE HOLLYWOOD MOVIES?

Generally a movie produced by an American film studio by definition is a Hollywood film. In the early days it meant that the films were literally made in the suburb of Hollywood but these days the term Hollywood is shorthand for the entire US film industry.“Hollywood” is a name used to describe the entire U.S. film industry, which started in the Los Angeles, California suburb of Hollywood. So a Hollywood movie would be an American movie.These days, a Hollywood film is any big-budget American movie. Hollywood was originally a ranch established in 1886, which became a farming village in 1903. Just seven years later, barns began to be converted into movie studios, and the rest is history.Hollywood usually refers to Hollywood, Los Angeles, a neighborhood in California. Hollywood, a metonym for the cinema of the United States.
